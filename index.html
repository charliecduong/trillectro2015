LAWN

Lawn ticket holders give you access to an expansive, grass-covered viewing area. Bring a blanket, lay out and get to know your neighbors! Plenty of bars, tasty food choices, and restrooms are conveniently located nearby.

Pavilion

Pavilion ticket holders provide access to open seating in the covered pavilion as well as lawn access. Get close to the action or take it easy in the grass. Just a few steps away from all the conveniences of the lawn area.
